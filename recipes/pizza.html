Pizza à la Freddy


Description

Pizza is everybodys favorite. Try out Freddy specialé it is appreached by italien families from times on!

Ingredients for 2 Persons

  • 500g Flour
  • 250ml Water
  • 1 Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Pinch of Pepper
For the pizza topping:
  • 1 Dose of Tomatosauce
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 pieces of Mushrooms
  • 4 pieces of sausage
  • 1 package of Mais
  • 1 piece of cheese
  • 2 pieces of pepperoni
  • 3 spoons of oil
  • 1 piece of garlic

How to make Freddys Pizza

  1. Preparation of pizza dough: Put the flour into a bowl. Mix it with warm water, salt, oil and sugar.
  2. Mix the dough until it's a flat surface. Let it wait for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough to a pizza. Put the tomato sauce on it. Cut the onions, mushrooms, sausage, mais, cheese and pepperoni and put it as topping on the pizza.
  4. Put the pizza dough together with the topping in the oven and wait for 15-20 min.
Est voila! Your pizza a la Freddy! Bon Appetit!
