Noodles with Tomato Sauce


Description

Noodles with Tomato Sauce is an absolute classic from the italien kitchen. You will love this dish. It can serve as a quick meal if you do not have time or as a regular dinner with your best ones!

Ingredients

  • 400g Noodles
  • 500g Tomatoes (passionated)
  • 1 medium sited Onion
  • some Tomatomark
  • 2 Eating Spoons Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • sour creme
  • Parmesan

How to make Noodles with Tomato Sauce?

  1. Cook spaghettis until they are able to chew
  2. In between you can cut the onions and cook them in your pan together with the oil
  3. Extinguish the onions with your tomaten mark
  4. During the cooking add some spices with salt and pepper and sour creme
  5. Last step is to pour off the water of the noodels and add the sauce
Bon Appetit!
