How to make Chicken Noodle Casserole

  1. The first step is to preheat the oven by 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Then place a large pot of water over the high heat and bring it to a boil. Cook the egg noodles for 8 minutes until they're slightly undercooked. They'll finish cooking in the oven.
  2. The second step is to saute the onions and garlic in a medium skillet over medium heat with some olive oil. Once tender, pour them into a medium mixing bowl along with the frozen veggies.
  3. The third step is to add both cans of soup, milk, and chicken broth to a large mixing bowl. Whisk that together until combined.
  4. The fourth step is to add the chicken, noodles, and veggies to the bowl with the soup mixture and stir to combine. Season it with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. The fifth step is then to spread the noodle mixture into an even layer in a greased 9-inch x 13-inch baking dish. Bake for 25 minutes until the top is slightly golden and the sauce is bubbling.
